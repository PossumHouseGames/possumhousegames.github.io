Basch is a classically trained musician who graduated with a degree in Audio Engineering in 2014. He has had a lifelong passion for Video Games and Video Game Music, with a childhood dream of being on the ending credits of a game. This passion for games has led him to be a part of the Roc Game Dev Team, the lead for music and audio with Possum House Games, and also an independent game developer.
